## Which Should You Choose?

### Choose Premium Complete System if:
- ✅ You want something that "just works"
- ✅ You value professional support
- ✅ You prefer ready-made solutions
- ✅ You don't want to handle technical setup
- ✅ You want a weatherproof enclosure included

### Choose Smart Camera Module if:
- ✅ You already have a bird box
- ✅ You enjoy DIY projects
- ✅ You're comfortable with basic electronics
- ✅ You like customizing your setup
- ✅ You want to save some money

### Choose DIY Component Build if:
- ✅ You're a maker or developer
- ✅ You want to learn about IoT/embedded systems
- ✅ You prefer open-source solutions
- ✅ You have soldering skills
- ✅ You can troubleshoot issues yourself
- ✅ You want maximum customization

## FAQ

**Q: Can I upgrade from Smart Module to Premium later?**
A: Not directly, but the camera module retains value and can be sold or repurposed.

**Q: Do DIY builders get any warranty?**
A: No warranty on self-built systems. Individual components may have manufacturer warranties.

**Q: Will you offer installation services?**
A: Not initially. We provide detailed guides. Professional installation may be available in future.

**Q: Can I buy multiple units?**
A: Yes! Bulk pricing available for 5+ units. Contact us for a quote.

**Q: What's included with technical support?**
A: Premium: Email support for setup, configuration, and troubleshooting. Does NOT include bird box installation or physical mounting assistance.

**Q: Can businesses/researchers buy these?**
A: Yes! Contact us for commercial/research pricing and volume discounts.
