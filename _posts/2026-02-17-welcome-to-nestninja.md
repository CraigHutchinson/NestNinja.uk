We're excited to announce the launch of the NestNinja project! 🎉

## What is NestNinja?

NestNinja is an open-source smart bird box camera system built on the powerful ESP32-P4 platform. Our goal is to make high-quality bird monitoring accessible to everyone - whether you're a hobbyist, educator, researcher, or conservationist.

## Why Another Bird Box Camera?

Great question! Here's what makes NestNinja different:

### 🏆 Premium Quality
We're building professional-grade bird monitoring systems with premium hardware, reliable software, and proper support.

### 🔓 Open Innovation
Firmware will be open-sourced once stable, enabling the maker community to build custom solutions. Choose between ready-made products or DIY builds.

### 💰 Options for Every Budget
- **£200-300** - Complete smart bird box system (estimated)
- **£100-150** - Smart camera module for existing boxes (estimated)  
- **£65-100** - DIY component build with community support

### 🔋 Low Power
PIR motion detection means the camera only wakes when birds are present. Perfect for battery or solar-powered installations.

### 🎥 High Quality
1080p HD video with H.264 hardware encoding, 160° fisheye lens, and night vision capabilities.

### 🏠 Privacy First
All data stored locally on SD card. No cloud required, no subscriptions, your data stays yours.

## Current Status

We're currently in the **architecture finalization phase**. This involves:

- Testing the ESP32-P4 + ESP32-C6 platform
- Integrating the OV5647 camera module
- Optimizing power management strategies
- Developing the core firmware architecture

**Development Status:** Firmware is currently private during initial development. We'll open-source it once we reach stable release (targeting Q3 2026).

Follow updates on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/CraigHutchinson/NestNinja) (will be public soon!).

## What's Next?

Our immediate priorities:

1. **Hardware Testing** - Validate all components work together
2. **Camera Driver** - Get the OV5647 capturing video
3. **Video Encoding** - Configure H.264 hardware encoder
4. **Power Management** - Implement PIR wake from deep sleep
5. **Basic Streaming** - Get video from camera to web browser
